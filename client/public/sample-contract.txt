1. SCOPE OF WORK

2. TIMELINE

notice or compensation to Client. Any feedback or bug report submitted
by Client is irrevocably assigned to Provider.

6. TERMINATION
Provider may terminate at any time on 24 hours' written notice.
Client may terminate only for Provider's uncured material breach
after a 180-day cure period, during which Client must continue to
pay all invoices. On termination by either party: all amounts paid
are retained by Provider as liquidated damages; any unpaid balance
accelerates and becomes immediately due; Provider has no obligation
to deliver source code, assets, or documentation.
